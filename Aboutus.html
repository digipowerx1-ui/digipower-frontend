“Our Evolution”

Digi Power X began as Digihost, focused primarily on sustainable Bitcoin mining. Recognizing the growing demand for energy, AI, and high-performance computing (HPC), we transformed into Digi Power X — a next-generation infrastructure company. Today, we combine owned energy resources with advanced modular data centers to power the future of AI, cloud, and blockchain.

Our team of infrastructure experts, AI researchers, and industry veterans came together to build something different—a platform that combines the flexibility of cloud computing with the performance of dedicated hardware.
